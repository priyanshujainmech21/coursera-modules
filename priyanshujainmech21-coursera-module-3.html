Samosa

Samosa is a nicely fried or cooked food.Its outer layer is created using maida which on frying makes it crunchy.It contains almost every kind of spices means a mioxture of spices,mixed with boiled potato.Special type of chutneys are prepared for this kind of food.It is the most favourite and usable food of India.IT can also be eaten with tomato ketchup.

Kachori

Kachori is a nicely fried or cooked food.It is filled with a type of pulse.Its outer layer is also created using maida which also makes it crunchy on frying.There are mainly two types of kachories: one with onion and other without onion.when mixed with special types of chutneys,it taste much heavenly.

Khaman

It is made of besan.It is too much soft and fluffy and tastes sour and sweet due to the mixture of spices and mustard.It is prepared using steam process.It is of yellow color.It can be eaten with ketchup.It is the most favourite dish of gujrati people or one of the most favourite and eaten by people all over the India.
